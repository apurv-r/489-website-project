2. Project Description 
Nexa is a web-based marketplace that connects people who have underutilized parking spaces at their homes or workplaces (such as garages, driveways, vacant lots) to those who are looking for affordable local parking without spending a large amount. Cities like Seattle are notorious for having high parking prices that can be a real burden for people trying to work in the city. This project is inspired by and will function similarly to websites like Airbnb, but for vehicle parking. Hosts can list their spaces with photos, dimensions, description tags, and availability windows. Drivers can then search via a map, filter by distance from their desired location, and book a guaranteed spot for a time period. 

This solves a problem for not just drivers looking for inexpensive and guaranteed parking, but also helps out home or business owners. Many such owners have empty driveways while they are at work or simply have unused lots at their business. Using Nexa, they can convert this dormant asset into something that provides them a passive income with little to no effort. 

3. User Roles
Who is Nexa for? It’s for people who might be traveling, and are looking for somewhere to park their car for a certain length of time. It could be for someone who is looking for parking near work or maybe a friend's house. It’s also for people who have parking space to their name but aren't making use of it. Really, Nexa is for two people: those looking for a place to park, and those looking to lease their place to park.

There will be three user roles. Admin, lessor, and lessee. 

The Admin role is designed for devs and moderators of the platform. With the admin role, you can view statistics such as what areas exhibit the highest/lowest demand for parking, what areas have the highest available parking, average parking lease cost, etc. The admin role would also handle lot verifications to ensure the website isn’t flooded with inaccurate listings.

The lessor role is designed for the user who wants to lease out their parking space. In this role, the user can make their space available by posting their parking space’s availability, location, and price. The lessor could set custom rates, such as $xx per xx hours. Finally, this user can view their posts, and see data such as views, and delete a post.

The lessee role is designed for the user who is booking for available parking. In this user role, the user can browse for available parking within a selected area. They can also view and manage their current leases, and save posts for later.

4. Expected pages
Home - Overview and search for parking spaces.


Login / Registration - Sign up or log in as Admin, Lessor, or Lessee.


Search / Listing - Browse available parking spaces via map or list.


Parking Details - Photos, dimensions, price, and availability.


Booking & Payment - Reserve and pay for a parking spot.


User Dashboard - View bookings (Lessee) or manage listings (Lessor).


Admin Dashboard - Monitor platform statistics, verify listings, manage users.


About Us - Platform mission and information.
