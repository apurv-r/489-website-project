About this space

A private, fully covered garage located just two blocks from the Capitol Hill light rail station. Perfect for daily commuters and visitors exploring the neighbourhood. The space fits standard and mid-size SUVs comfortably. Access is via a keypad — code sent upon booking confirmation. Security cameras are monitored 24/7 and the garage is well-lit with ample turning room.

EV charging (Level 2, J1772) is available at no extra cost. The garage door height is 2.1 m — please check clearance before booking if you drive a tall vehicle.
